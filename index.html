Pancreatic cancer is a disease in which cancerous cells form in the tissues of the pancreas.
The American Cancer Society reports that only about 23% of patients with cancer of the exocrine pancreas are still living one year after diagnosis. Only about 4% are still living five years after being diagnosed.

The money will go towards hospitals, and clinics to help them continue giving great treatment. It will also help to fund organizations working with fighting this disease to help spread awareness internationally. Let's all stay aware and live health. Thank you.
